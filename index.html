In 2015,National Review published a line chart on "average avenual global temperature in Fahrenheit", which was called "the bad chart heard round the (graphics) world".

This is the chart we should all think about when someone says line charts should always have a y-axis starting at zero. A change of even one degree in the average global temperature is significant, but starting this chart at zero makes it look miniscule.
